Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

LockedHQ respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the LockedHQ mobile application.

  • To provide core app functionality
  • To authenticate users and secure accounts

Health Data

Health data is accessed through Apple HealthKit only after you grant permission. This data is used solely for app functionality and is not used for advertising, marketing, or tracking.

Data Sharing

LockedHQ does not sell your personal data and does not share it with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Data Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect your data.
