How it works

Commitments people actually honor.

LockedHQ uses real money to turn intentions into follow-through. You don’t bet. You don’t speculate. You commit — or you don’t get paid.

1
Create a commitment
Define the rule, the deadline, and the stake. Showing up, completing a task, or sticking to a plan — you decide what counts.
2
Lock in money
Participants put money on the line. Funds are held securely via Stripe while the commitment is active.
3
Resolve the outcome
When the commitment ends, funds are distributed based on the rules. LockedHQ takes a small fee only when money is withdrawn.
Why it works

Because motivation fades. Consequences don’t.

LockedHQ isn’t about rewards or gamification. It works because people behave differently when there’s something real at stake.

No chasing people
Money is locked up front. No reminders. No awkward follow-ups.
Clear rules
Everyone agrees to the same outcome before committing. No debates later.
Real accountability
Social pressure fades. Financial consequences don’t.
Money & fees

Simple, transparent money flow.

LockedHQ only makes money when you successfully withdraw. No subscriptions. No fees just to participate.

Deposits
  • Paid by card or Apple Pay
  • Held securely via Stripe
  • No LockedHQ fee on deposits
Withdrawals
  • 5% platform fee on withdrawal
  • Net amount shown before you confirm
  • Payouts typically take 1–7 business days
FAQ

Quick answers.

Is this gambling?
No. LockedHQ is for commitments with predefined outcomes, not chance or odds.
Who holds the money?
Payments are processed and held via Stripe until withdrawal.
How does LockedHQ make money?
A 5% fee is taken only when funds are withdrawn.
